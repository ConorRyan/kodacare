About Me

Animal-assited therapist and physiotherapist

Hi! My name is Perrine and I live in the Ottawa area. I worked 7 years as a physiotherapist before following my dream of working with my dog as my partner by doing two years of intensive training in animal-assisted therapy and being certified by the Corporation des zoothérapeutes du Québec (CZQ).

My qualifications:

  • Master's degree in physiotherapy.
  • 7 years of experience as a physiotherapist.
  • 2 years zootherapy bootcamp.
  • Worked with multiple Ottawa-based zootherapists.

Therapy is my passion and I believe in tailoring my approach to each individual as everyone has different needs and wishes.

Koda has been profesionnally trained for over 18 months to be able to handle all types of situations which allows him to be versatile and assist in lots of different types of sessions, having fun all the while!
