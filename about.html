About Me

Animal-assited therapist and physiotherapist

Hi! My name is Perrine and I live in Ottawa. I worked 7 years as a physiotherapist before following my dream of working with my dog as my partner by doing two years of intensive training in animal-assisted therapy and being certified by the Corporation des zoothérapeutes du Québec (CZQ).

My qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Physiotherapy (Belgian, currently in the process of being accredited in Canada)
  • Experience as a physiotherapist since 2015
  • 2 years animal-assisted therapy bootcamp
  • Worked with multiple Ottawa-based zootherapists

Therapy is my passion and I believe in tailoring my approach to each individual to cater to different needs and wishes.
