About Me

Animal-assisted therapist and physiotherapist

Hi, I'm Perrine. I started my career as a physiotherapist specializing in neurological rehabilitation. Then I took an intensive two-year training course in animal-assisted therapy to integrate it into my patients' sessions. I'm also a member of the Corporation des zoothérapeutes du Québec (CZQ).

My qualifications:

  • Master's degree in Physiotherapy (Belgian, currently in the process of being accredited in Canada)
  • Experience as a physiotherapist since 2015
  • 2 years animal-assisted therapy bootcamp
  • Worked with multiple Ottawa-based animal-assisted therapists

Since he was a baby, Koda has been trained to accompany you during your sessions, whatever your age or situation! For more information, click here.

But the real question is: What do you need?

As a professional, I'll be there to encourage you, support you and open your eyes to your potential, inviting you to push your limits a little further each time and helping you find a balance that suits you. It won't always be easy, but I'll give you all the tools you need to succeed. I'll walk beside you and help you up when you need it. I'll always try to be as close as possible to what's best for you. I'll get you moving in body and mind.
