Cognitive and Emotional Development

Would you change anything in your life? Yes? Then come on, let's go! It's time to get closer to the life that suits you, don't you think? It's always the perfect time to take care of yourself!

This is aimed towards adults and children.

How can this form of therapy help you?

  • Better manage your emotions
  • Help deal with your stress
  • Help mitigate learning delay
  • Gain confidence to make changes
  • Assist children with school or relational difficulties
  • Difficult times in life

Motricity Development

Before starting working with Koda, I worked as a physio rehab specialist in adult and pediatric neurology. I've met people who are both incredibly strong and fragile in the face of life's injustices, in a body that no longer responds to commands. Together, we can fight for greater autonomy.

With my furry friend, we're here to give you all the tools you need to live life to the fullest!

For any condition impairing motricity:

  • Spinal injuries
  • Brain injuries
  • Parkinson and related syndromes
  • Motor delays
  • Cerebral palsy
  • Down syndrome and genetic syndromes
  • ADHD/Autism
  • Strokes
  • Neuropathy
  • and more!
