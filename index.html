Why should I do Animal-Assisted Therapy?

A dog is bubbly, motivational and gentle, but not only that!

In addition to these benefits, when he is well trained, he is also an incredible therapeutic ally to help you evolve, take action, take care of yourself and others while respecting yourself regardless of your age or condition!

Who is it for?

  • Anyone looking to gain support, self-confidence, or manage stress.
  • People with motor, social, or learning difficulties.
  • Children wanting to participate in an novel activity, a magical moment, alone or in a group.
  • Professionals working in the field of health or education, looking for educational and playful ways of working at the same time.
  • And more, feel free to contact me to discuss!
