Animal-Assisted Therapy in the Ottawa area

Are you looking for a fresh, more engaging, and efficient form of therapy? I'm Perrine, a zootherapist and physiotherapist. I'm here to offer you quality services that combine a warm, relaxed approach with professional expertise.

Contact Me

Therapeutic Support

To provide therapeutic support in managing emotions and stress; to gain self-confidence, learn to set limits and create your bubble.

Motor Exercises

I'm also a physiotherapist specializing in neurological disorders so I can help improve motor abilities.

Recreational Activities

To give children the benefit of a fun, original activity to help them learn while playing, or discover the world of dogs and learn how they work in a fun way.

How Animal-Assisted Therapy can help you

It is a great alternative to help in a number of situations where more traditional therapy isn't getting the results you want.

Who is it for?

  • Anyone with motor, social or learning difficulties.
  • Anyone wishing to offer or participate in an novel activity, a magical moment, alone or in a group.
  • Any other professional working in the field of health or education, looking for educational and playful ways of working at the same time.
  • Or anything else, feel free to reach out!

Koda - my dog
